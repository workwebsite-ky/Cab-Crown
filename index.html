FAQ

Frequently Asked

How do I book a Cab Crown ride? +
You can book via our contact form, email us at contactglanders@gmail.com, or call us directly. We aim to confirm your booking within 30 minutes.
Is there surge pricing during peak hours? +
No. Cab Crown offers fixed, transparent pricing. The fare quoted at booking is the fare you pay — no surprises, no surge.
Can I pre-book for the next day or week? +
Absolutely. We encourage advance bookings and can schedule rides weeks ahead. For regular corporate accounts we can set up recurring schedules.
Do you offer corporate accounts? +
Yes. We offer dedicated corporate accounts with monthly invoicing, designated account managers, and priority fleet access for your team.
What if my flight is delayed? +
We track all incoming flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver will automatically adjust their arrival time at no extra charge.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and bank transfers for corporate clients. Payment can be made in the vehicle or pre-paid online.
